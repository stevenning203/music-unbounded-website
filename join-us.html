Join Our Team as a Volunteer
We are currently looking for enthusiastic teachers who are passionate about sharing their craft. As a volunteer at M.U., you will meet regularly with your students to help them learn their instrument while following a curriculum.

Responsibilities:

  • You will show up to lessons on time.
  • You will communicate with your student to create an open learning environment.
  • You will work with the rest of our team in order to help improve lessons.

Perks of Volunteering at M.U.:

  • Teacher training
  • Volunteer experience and hours
  • Letter of Appreciation
  • References for future job or university applications

In addition to filling out the application form, you may be required to attend a short interview that includes a music skill test. If you'd like to join our team, please fill out the application below.
